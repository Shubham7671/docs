## Welcome to QuickReel API

QuickReel provides a comprehensive suite of AI-powered video processing APIs that help you create, edit, and enhance video content programmatically. Our platform combines cutting-edge AI technology with robust API infrastructure to deliver professional-grade video processing capabilities.

## What We Offer

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="AI Clipping" icon="scissors">
    Automatically convert long-form videos into engaging short-form content
    optimized for social media.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI AutoCrop" icon="crop">
    Detect active speakers in horizontal videos and intelligently crop to
    vertical 9:16 HD format.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI Text to Video" icon="closed-captioning">
    Generate engaging videos from text prompts with voice narration, visuals,
    subtitles, and background music.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI BGM" icon="music">
    Add context-aware background music to enhance video engagement and emotional
    impact.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI B-rolls" icon="film">
    Insert relevant B-roll footage based on video context for
    professional-looking content.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Subtitles API" icon="closed-captioning">
    Add animated subtitles to videos, improving accessibility and engagement.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
